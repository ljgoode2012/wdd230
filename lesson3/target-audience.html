Scenarios

Scenario 1

Grace is the manager of a well known, big chain store located in the community. She needs to improve the store’s relationship with the local community. As a member of the chamber, she goes on the website, scrolls to the bottom of the homepage and clicks on the contact information page. She finds the contact information and reaches out to the appropriate person to discuss sponsorship opportunities for future events.

Scenario 2

Jorge is a local small business owner. He needs help increasing his T-shirt design business’s presence in the community. He gets on the website and sees the “join” button in the hero image. He clicks on it and is taken to a page that explains the benefits of joining the chamber of commerce and presents the form for joining. He fills out the form and clicks submit. Jorge has found a simple way to join the chamber and receive support for growing his business.

Scenario 3

Jennifer is a dentist for a local practice. She is looking for a way to network with other businesses so she can hopefully advertise with them and reach a wider audience base. She gets on the website and scrolls down to see the event slideshow. She makes note of several events that give her the opportunity to build relationships with other local businesses. She enters the dates into her calendar.

Scenario 4

Kim is a website developer. She needs a way to reach out to as many businesses as possible about their web presence. Kim goes on the website and clicks on the navigation button. She sees the link to the directory page and clicks on it. There she sees the websites and contact information for many of the chamber members. She goes through the list and looks for websites in need of improvement. She then reaches out to those businesses in hopes of consulting for them.

Scenario 5

Rob is a local restaurant owner. He would like to open a second location in the community but he is unsure of the current demand. He goes on the website and sees the join button. He reads that the chamber can help him with services such as analyzing market trends. He fills out the form presented to him and clicks submit. He clicks the navigation button and clicks on the contact page. Rob reaches out to the chamber for market analytical services.
