Liya's Cookies Grand Opening!

Welcome Liya's Cookies to the Madison Community! They opened their doors on Monday November 14th to an eager crowd ready for their first try of Liya's unique, delicious flavors. A madison resident, born and raised, Liya has refined her cookie recipies over the years and served them at various city events over the years. Now you can grab a mouthwatering cookie whenever the craving hits at 987 Sweet Ln!
