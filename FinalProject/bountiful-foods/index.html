Providing Healthy Options for Your Community

Meet Us in Los Angeles

Come grab a refreshing drink at one of our food trucks that travels the LA area! With our highly mobile food truck, we can bring farm fresh fruit baskets and produce right to your neighboorhood. We love serving our customers from the shopping district in downtown LA all the way to sunny, Santa Monica Bay. Meet us at popular events like the 4th of July Fireworks Spectacular, the LA County Fair, or the LA Film Festival and we will be sure to have something to match the occasion.

Meet Us in San Diego

The beautiful city of San Diego has been a joy to serve over the last 5 years. Find our food truck at Mission Beach Park and Ocean Beach City Beach with a fresh smoothie to cool you off while relaxing at the beach or people watching along the boardwalk. Just visiting? We'd love to give you a taste of California while you explore animals around the world at Sea World and the San Diego Zoo. We offer the best of the produce California has to offer and we bring right to you.

Visit Us in Carlsbad

Carlsbad is where we first began our mission to bring healthy, fresh food to the warm and welcoming cities of Southern California. Come visit us at our storefront location at 123 Appletree Ln and have fun designing a drink that fits your mood. On your way to Legoland for a day of family fun? Stop by for a healthy treat made from the fruit of the local organic farms. Providing clean and fresh produce to the families of Carlsbad has been our pleasure for the last 15 years.
