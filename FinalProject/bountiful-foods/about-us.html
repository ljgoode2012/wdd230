About Us

Bountiful Foods was established in 2007. We began by gathering fruit baskets from local farmers and delivering them in bulk to neighborhoods in need of affordable, fresh produce. We became so passionate about providing healthy options from trustworthy local farmers, that we have expanded our business over the last 15 years. Our mission has been to spread the joy of healthy living to as many people in Southern California as possible.
